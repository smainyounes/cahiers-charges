About Nordex

A Trusted Name in Men's Casual Clothing Supply

Nordex Supply Co. is a Manchester-based wholesale clothing distributor with over a decade of experience supplying modern casual men's fashion to hundreds of retailers across the UK and Europe.

We work directly with clothing manufacturers to offer consistent stock, competitive bulk pricing, and a trend-forward catalogue that keeps our retail partners ahead of the market.

Consistent, large-volume stock availability year-round
Trend-driven casual designs refreshed every season
Trusted by 500+ clothing stores and online retailers
Fast dispatch with tracked delivery across UK & Europe
