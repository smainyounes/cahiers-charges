Company Story

Built Around the Needs of Modern Retailers

Arkon Supply Group is a UK-based wholesale clothing distributor that has been supplying modern casual men's fashion to retailers, boutiques, and online sellers for over a decade.

We work directly with manufacturers to maintain consistent stock on our most popular lines, offer competitive bulk pricing, and ensure our partners always have access to the latest casual styles.
